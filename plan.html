Plan Your Trip to Darjeeling

Suggested itineraries, best time to visit, and handy tips.

Best Time to Visit

  • March – May: Pleasant weather, tea gardens lush and green.
  • October – November: Clear skies & views of Kanchenjunga.
  • December – February: Cold, possible snow on higher peaks; quiet season.

Suggested 3-Day Itinerary

  1. Day 1: Tiger Hill sunrise, Ghoom Monastery, Tea Garden visit, local market.
  2. Day 2: Toy Train ride (Darjeeling Himalayan Railway), Padmaja Naidu Zoo, Himalayan Mountaineering Institute.
  3. Day 3: Rock Garden, Japanese Peace Pagoda, relax at a tea estate and stroll.

Travel Tips

  • Carry warm layers even in summer mornings and evenings.
  • Book sunrise visits early; Tiger Hill attracts crowds.
  • Respect local customs and the fragile mountain ecosystem.
